Our founders, Jimmy and Tim or
Lashell&Noe as they are more commonly known as,
always dreamt of creating great music
in their childhood.

Both of them started out as indie artists
and later formed the band Delta, breaking into
the Top20 Young Musicians Of The
World List in 1983.
As successful musicians, both then forayed
into the Audio market with Lashell&Noe.
Lashell&Noe has done groundbreaking work
for both musicians and casual music lovers alike.

Lashell&Noe actively support athletes
and indie artists and bands through various
support and partner programs and
outreaches.
MUSIC IS IN OUR DNA

Audio products.
Our philosophy is that Good Music
eliminates geographical boundaries
and unites the world.
